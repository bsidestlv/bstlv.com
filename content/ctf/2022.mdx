# BSidesTLV 2022 CTF

## Event Overview

The BSidesTLV 2022 CTF marked a triumphant return to our hybrid format, combining remote participation with on-site activities. This year's competition featured our most extensive challenge set yet, with 26 challenges across 7 categories.

## Competition Statistics

- **Participation:** 681 registered teams with 1,158 users from 7,963 IP addresses
- **Challenges:** 26 unique challenges across 7 categories  
- **Solutions:** 678 solved challenges total
- **Active Teams:** 190 teams solved at least 1 challenge
- **Duration:** Extended 48+ hour competition (June 27-29, 2022)

## Challenge Categories

The 2022 CTF featured an expanded set of 7 categories with 26 total challenges:

- **Cryptography** - Advanced encryption and hash-based puzzles
- **Web Security** - Complex application security vulnerabilities
- **Binary Exploitation** - Advanced reverse engineering and pwn
- **Forensics** - Digital investigation and steganography
- **Blockchain** - Smart contract security (featuring Solidity challenges)
- **Miscellaneous** - Creative and unique challenge formats
- **OSINT** - Open source intelligence and investigation

## Technical Infrastructure

### Advanced Features:
- **Containerized Challenges** - Scalable and isolated environments
- **Session Management** - BSidesTLV cookie system for challenge persistence
- **Auto-cleanup** - Hourly maintenance for file-based challenges
- **Global Accessibility** - Optimized for international participation

## Competition Rules

### Key Guidelines:
1. **Hybrid Participation** - Remote allowed, but physical presence required for prizes
2. **Team Structure** - Recommended size: 5 players (maximum: 10)
3. **Fair Play** - Strict anti-cheating measures with disqualification authority
4. **Session Persistence** - Proper cookie handling required for web challenges
5. **Extended Access** - Challenges available ~1 week post-competition for writeups
