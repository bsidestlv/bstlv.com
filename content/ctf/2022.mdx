# BSidesTLV 2022 CTF

## Event Overview

The BSidesTLV 2022 CTF marked another milestone in our annual cybersecurity competition series, bringing together security enthusiasts for a day of challenging problem-solving.

## Competition Statistics

- **Participation:** 75 teams with 220+ players
- **Challenges:** 16 unique challenges
- **Duration:** Full day competition
- **Format:** Jeopardy-style CTF

## Challenge Categories

The 2022 CTF featured challenges across the following categories:

- **Cryptography** - Classical and modern encryption challenges
- **Web Security** - Application security vulnerabilities
- **Binary Exploitation** - Reverse engineering and binary analysis
- **Forensics** - Digital forensics and incident response
- **Network Security** - Network protocols and traffic analysis

## Event Highlights

The 2022 edition saw increased participation compared to previous years, with teams demonstrating impressive technical skills across all challenge categories. The competition fostered collaboration and learning within the cybersecurity community.
